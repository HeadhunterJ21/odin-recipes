The Perfect Basic Burger

Description

These burger patties are made with ground beef and an easy bread crumb mixture. Nothing beats a simple hamburger on a warm sumer evening. Pile these burgers with your favorite condiments and pop open a cool drink!

Ingredients

  1. 1 large egg
  2. 0.5 teaspoon salt
  3. 0.5 teaspoon ground black pepper
  4. 1 pound ground beef
  5. 0.5 cup fine dry bread crumbs
